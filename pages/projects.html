Operational Footprint

Environmental Projects & Deliverables

A detailed showcase of our six core regional interventions addressing water security, sustainable sanitation, and female-led agrarian cooperative projects.

Water & WASH Linkage

Anaji Clean Water Borehole & Filtration Hub

Installing deep solar pumping rigs paired with multi-layer sand filter basins to replace creek harvesting for schoolchildren.

📍 Location: Anaji, outer Takoradi District

📈 Impact: Yields 15,000L daily of biological clear water, serving 2,500+ pupils.

Hygienic Sanitation

Takoradi Market Circle Composting Complex

Constructing an eco-friendly composting public toilet with a rainwater collectors to prevent water-borne disease outbreaks in busy trading centers.

📍 Location: Takoradi Central Market Circle

📈 Impact: Designed to serve 800+ micro-merchants daily with bio-waste capture.

Agro-Cooperative

Kwesimintsim Female Farmers Drip Pipeline

Channeling shallow groundwater streams to modular gravity-fed drip hoses, securing regional vegetable farmlands.

📍 Location: Kwesimintsim Outskirts

📈 Impact: Safeguards active cropping yields for 180 female-run cooperative plots.

Hygiene & Advocacy

Effiakuma Schools WASH Action Campaign

Integrating handwashing points and providing clean hygiene workshops for children to halt recurring rotavirus.

📍 Location: Effiakuma Municipal High Schools

📈 Impact: Set up 15 high-volume washbasins, eliminating seasonal water illnesses.

Coastal Salitration

Sekondi Beach Bio-Sand Filter Wells

Building localized sand bank structures to filtrate saline and high-iron soil lagoons, ensuring domestic access.

📍 Location: Sekondi Fishing Beach Shorelines

📈 Impact: Will bring emergency backup freshwater reserves to 3,000 fishing households.

Solar Technology

Kojokrom Solar Micro-Purification Kiosk

A self-contained solar-powered vending machine treating groundwater via charcoal adsorption, managed by a local youth council.

📍 Location: Kojokrom Railway Station Marketplace

📈 Impact: Generates self-sustaining operational revenues while supplying cheap water.
