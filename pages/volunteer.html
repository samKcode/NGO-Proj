Why Volunteer with KCTF?

Building community autonomy requires more than just casting pipes - it requires enthusiastic, dedicated community links. As a registered volunteer with Samuel Korankye's team, you acquire valuable field experience in water quality analysis, compost management, and sociology.

02

Boreholes Quality Analyst

Work with the local hydrology directors taking samples for bacterial and mineral screenings.

03

Organic Farm Mobilizer

Assist municipal female co-operatives with building eco-composting arrays on vegetable beds.
