Student Initiative & Sand-filter Prototyping

Under the technical guidance of founder Samuel Korankye, the initiative started as a group of computer science and environmental engineering students at Takoradi Technical University. We gathered resources to cast 50 concrete bio-sand cylinders, carrying clean drinking sources to schools immediately bordering outer Takoradi.

Solar Deep-Casing Wells Commissioned

Integrating efficient solar deep mechanical well installations which can reach untouched deep aquifers (80m deep). This successfully bypasses contaminated rain runoff grids, completely eradicating bacterial diseases within our partner villages.

Integrated Green Agricultural Co-ops

Setting up direct piping systems linked with organic crop yards. This allows mothers to fertilize agricultural yields utilizing the sanitary compost generated from our closed composting restrooms, multiplying economic returns.
