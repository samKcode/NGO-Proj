Our Direct Purpose

Why Korankye Community Transformation Foundation Exists

Founded in Takoradi, KCTF works at the intersection of local community empowerment and public healthcare sanitation. Clean water shortages burden hundreds of schoolgoing children and mothers. By executing localized hydro-geological water well projects and building modern agro-cooperative centers, we secure robust clean-drinking and financial futures.

Water & WASH Linkage

Anaji Clean Water Borehole & Filtration Hub

Engineering a solar-powered borehole well coupled with sand bio-filters. Yields 15,000 liters of treated drinking water daily, eliminating water scarcity.

📍 Anaji Community, Takoradi, Western Region

📈 Yield: Provides bacteria-free tap water to 2,500+ residents.

Hygienic Sanitation

Takoradi Market Circle Sanitation Station

Building a composting flush toilet and greywater marshgarden filtration system utilizing local collected rainwater to preserve market hygiene.

📍 Market Circle, Takoradi Administrative Area

📈 Yield: Will support 800+ micro-traders daily, models biocombustion.
